ReHRDD Salience Analysis

1. Scope

How many relevant rightsholders could be impacted?

2. Scale

How seriously could rightsholders be impacted?

3. Remediability

To what extent can the rightsholder be restored to the same or equivalent position as before the impact?

4. Likelihood

How likely is it for the impact to occur?

5. Conflict

Potential harmful impact on conflict (see: Q 1.6)?
